## The Situation

A surgical device company with a cleared product needed to enter 6 new metro markets simultaneously. Their 4-person field team had no market intelligence, no mapped provider networks, and no prioritized accounts.

The traditional approach — using a sales consultant to build territory maps manually — would have taken 9 months and cost more than the BMA engagement.

## What We Built

We deployed the Togari platform to deliver complete market intelligence for all 6 markets in under 2 weeks.

The output for each market included:

- Complete maps of all surgical facilities with facility type, volume indicators, and contact information
- Provider profiles for every surgical director, OR manager, and department chief
- Referral network graphs showing which physicians drove device adoption at each major health system
- Facility scores ranking all 1,500+ targets by likelihood to adopt
- Territory designs concentrating each rep's focus on the top 80 facilities by conversion probability

## The Results

The team launched in all 6 markets in 11 weeks — compared to a 9-month industry average. Four pilot agreements were signed within 60 days of field launch.

The clinical champion outreach drove a 31% reply rate from cold — more than 3x what the team had achieved in existing markets using traditional approaches.
