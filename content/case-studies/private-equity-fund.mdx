## The Situation

A middle-market private equity firm with $2.4B AUM came to BMA with a fundamental challenge: 90% of their deal flow came through banker intermediaries, leaving them perpetually late to competitive processes.

The opportunity they were missing was enormous: millions of founder-owned businesses in their target verticals that never hired advisors and would consider a direct conversation with the right PE firm.

## What We Built

We designed and deployed a full-stack deal sourcing engine over 90 days that transformed how their BD team operates.

The system included:

- A proprietary ICP model for founder-owned businesses using 15 firmographic and technographic criteria
- Automated enrichment of 40,000 existing CRM records with verified ownership, leadership contacts, and business signals
- A deal signal monitoring system tracking 180+ trigger events across 12,000 target companies
- Multi-touch outreach sequences for cold founder outreach with PE-specific personalization
- A Clay workflow that auto-enriches new company additions and routes to the right BD partner

## The Results

The impact was measurable within 60 days of launch. Deal flow velocity tripled, data accuracy improved dramatically, and the BD team reclaimed 60% of their time from manual research.

Most importantly, the firm closed two proprietary deals in the first 6 months that they would never have found through traditional channels.
